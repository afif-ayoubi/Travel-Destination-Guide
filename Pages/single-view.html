Island

An island is a piece of land that is surrounded by water on all sides. Islands vary greatly in size, from tiny outcrops barely large enough to support a single tree to massive land masses like Greenland or Australia. They can be found in oceans, seas, lakes, and rivers around the world.
East Java offers a diverse array of attractions and experiences that promise adventure, cultural immersion, and relaxation. Nature enthusiasts can embark on exhilarating hikes to witness the awe-inspiring landscapes of Mount Bromo and Mount Ijen, where they can marvel at volcanic craters and surreal vistas. The region's stunning beaches, such as those in Banyuwangi and Malang, provide opportunities for sunbathing, surfing, and snorkeling amidst pristine waters and golden sands
